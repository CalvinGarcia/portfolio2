The approach for this project was simple. The front page showcases new sales happening currently, a podcast and a comic shop locator to better serve the audience. The color scheme is a tribute to golden age comic book printing methods that utilized red, blue, and yellow to showcase the color of heroes or protagonists in those comic books. The title “The Web” is a play not only on networking, but an homage of sorts to one of the greatest super heroes ever, Spider-Man.
