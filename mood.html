In The Mood For...

"In The Mood For" is a small local sweets business. They specialize in Flower cakes and Macaroons. The business is Asian owned and operated, and was looking for a clean and modern approach to Chinese ink and water color paintings. Taking inspiration from the logo of the film “In the Mood for Love”, I added in the touches of red often found in ancient Chinese Ink based paintings.
